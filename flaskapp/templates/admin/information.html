Types of Insurance Coverage

Learn about different insurance coverage options for boda-bodas:

  • Third-Party Insurance: Covers damage to third-party vehicles or property in case of an accident caused by the insured rider.
  • Comprehensive Insurance: Provides coverage for both third-party damages and damage to the insured rider's motorcycle.
  • Personal Accident Insurance: Offers financial support in case of injuries or death resulting from an accident.

How to Choose the Right Insurance

Consider the following factors when selecting an insurance policy:

  1. Coverage Scope: Assess the extent of coverage provided by each insurance type.
  2. Cost: Compare premium rates and choose an affordable option that meets your needs.
  3. Insurance Company Reputation: Research and select a reliable insurance provider with a good track record.
  4. Claim Process: Understand the claim procedure and duration for processing claims.
